## Section 3 — Session Analysis

### Narrative Summary

Dawn after the gate attack. [[Lt. Hargraven]] ordered a sweep of [[Hampshire]] — the village outside the fort, hit hardest — for survivors and intel: *"If you find anyone alive, bring them home."* There were no survivors. Bodies were drained bloodless ([[Feral Vampire Thrall]] kills) or crushed and half-eaten ([[Beast Thrall]] kills). [[Vega Bloodroot]] staked the dead and piled them for burning. A victim's locket held a picture of two children — and **no children were among the dead anywhere**. They were carried out.

**[[Valerian Hellebore]] found his parents mauled in his childhood home.** His scream carried across the village; [[Zelda Z Whipper]] hauled him out by the finger, [[Deanna Smith-Wesson]] covered the bodies. He spent the session swinging between gallows humor ("More alive than my parents") and channeled rage.

[[Samothy Smith-Wesson]]'s Detect Magic found a single set of magically-auraed footprints — pointed shoes, slight heel, large — touring the village and vanishing mid-step. His nat-20 History (27) recalled the aura as **light blue**; Hargraven identified the signature: a **Beast Master** (Vampire Masters read dark red; theory — Beast Masters are closer to human). ⚑ Relation to [[The Beast Binder]] pending DM.

War room: supplies dangerously low, northern farms gone, watchtowers empty, unnatural night fog. Three sorties: the party → **the old hospital** (medical supplies); others → eastern merchant district and northern farms; rendezvous in 2 days. Backstory drop: **Vega and Flux jumped three training classes** to graduate with this group — Vega set the physical records, Flux the stealth record.

[[Bobby]] (dwarf master armorer) **silvered the party's arsenal** (silver stops vampire self-healing), issued silver arrows/bullets, wooden stakes, a **Bag of Holding** — then barred the door and privately commissioned them: retrieve a rumored **magical book** from the hospital ("you'll know it when you see it — it's got a presence"). Also wanted: jewels for castle protection, and **emeralds — the only way to kill a Beastmaster**. [[Abigail]] (elf alchemist, carrying a years-long torch for Bobby's mustache) provided 6 superior potions + Elvish rope and commissioned **nirnroot** from the hospital pharmacy.

At the hospital (2 hours east): Valerian's curiosity triggered **3 blind beast thralls** — eyeless hunters tracking sound and heat. Brutal fight: Valerian knocked prone, took 31; Vega ended one with a **47-damage nat-20 crit** ("absolutely liquefy him"); Valerian's **Daylight on a rock** backed the heat-sensing beasts off; Zelda's Cure Wounds + **Lunar Vitality** patched Valerian; and Valerian executed the last one with Shocking Grasp — for his parents. Short rest in the ER; second floor and basement (morgue) remain.

*Table notes: DM parented kids to bed mid-combat; Taylor asked players to announce rolls aloud; Vega's item finalized as the Eldritch Claw Tattoo.*
