## Summary

**Crypt wrap-up.** Inside the welded armor, behind the neck: a **dried-blood alchemy seal, damaged** ([[Flux]] perception 19). Religious in symbology (Religion 17) — and [[Valerian Hellebore]] (Arcana 18) reads it in five seconds: **the seal trapped a soul inside the armor**. It's broken now; the soul is gone. [[Deanna Smith-Wesson]] copies the legible fragment into her (non-magical) notebook ⚑. Walking out of the church, the party sees **a tall pale figure in a long coat watching from a rooftop — he smiles, then vanishes into thin air.**

**The assignment.** [[Lt. Hargraven]] heads back to the castle; the party goes book-hunting for Sun Shard / relic lore at the **Grand Library of the Veiled Dawn** — "last time we sent scouts through, it was completely empty" (Vega: "I don't believe that, but okay"). Correct: it's torch-lit, active, and secretly run by the **CULT OF THE PALE WORLD** — human vampire-worshipers who guard forbidden knowledge and destroy anything hinting at vampire origins or how to bring back the sun.

**Cult hierarchy** (group History — Samothy 19, Flux 21): priests/monks → **bishops** (the [[The Beast Binder|Beast Tamer]]-types; 2 handmaidens) → **cardinals** (shapeshifting vampires, more sun-tolerant, passable as human; 7 handmaidens) → **the Pope** (deals directly with the vampires; unlimited handmaidens). Area bishop: **Aloysius — "Al"** — a psychopath. The restricted-section keeper: **Gregory**, a bottom-rung priest, *away for two days, no one knows where* ⚑. A **cardinal** oversees the region — name and face unknown, tracked only by "very, very gruesome religious murders" ⚑ (the rooftop figure?).

**The way in.** Deanna's renewable magic paper becomes a letter from the bishop — and the door cultist (**Josh**, "you're a G," fist-bump) waves the whole party through, too scared of Al to ask questions. Three floors, ~15 cultists ("not counting any vampire thralls or beast thralls — and now that I've said something, I won't tell you if there are"), and a hard house rule: **NO MAGIC** — enforced by ear (verbal components). Valerian's test cantrip: instant "*shh, no magic.*"

**The restricted section** (3rd floor): iron gate, electrified **magic bars** (Mr. Cat's nat-1 con save: 17 damage, flung into Samothy for 11), a **3×6-pin lock**, keyed only by the **Watch Captain** — a stern enforcer with resting bitch face who "did 20 years in Sing Sing." Three options live: pick it, pickpocket the key, or the **hidden bookshelf entrance** — red/green/blue tomes into the correct 3 of 8 slots (wrong slot rings a chime; worn triggers: slots 1, 3, 5, 8).

**The long con.** Samothy eyeballs the key (investigation dirty 20) and spends an hour fabricating a replica. Flux scouts the 3rd floor and pulls three books with a **26 investigation**: a dusty deep-red tome mentioning **Broken Shards**, a "…sun" book, a "vampire" book — then growl-checks a guard-monk via Valerian's gold-flash intimidation (dirty 20). The Watch Captain comes hunting for "Deanna, the leader" — so **Vega trips him** (dex 14: "they really need to stop mopping these floors"), then flirt-escorts him in circles around the first floor while Deanna hides. Valerian finds the **green book** (investigation 18 + the slot-wear clue); Barrett finds the **blue book** (whispering in an unknown language) in a study — next to **a book bound in human skin, nose indentation and all** (taken. Obviously).

**Everything goes wrong beautifully.** The replica key: Flux turns it — **nat-20 con save**, dex-24 jump-back, key disintegrates ("Mr. Stark, I don't feel so good"). Samothy slots the books red-green-blue — wrong order, the shelf reacts. And Vega, *done*, *Rages*: greataxe 25/17 + 23/21 (eating 9 + 4 electric feedback), Valerian caps it with a **nat-20 Starry Wisp for 28** — the doors blow open and **every soul in the library is now alerted**.

**CLIFFHANGER:** DM ends to build the encounter — **~15 combatants**. "Everybody make sure you have your second characters ready." Chase has A Plan he won't say aloud. Epilogue (Jill, post-table): while they fight, **Flux loots the restricted section for the relic lore.**
