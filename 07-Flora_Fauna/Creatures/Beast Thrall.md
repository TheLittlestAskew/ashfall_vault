# Beast Thrall

> First encountered: [[Session 02 — Ashes of the Living]] (3 slain — the blind variant). Distinct from the [[Feral Vampire Thrall]]: ferals are human-shaped and drain their kills; beast thralls are **massive** and crush/maul/eat them.

## Description

Large creatures ("much bigger than Zelda" — popping them with a toothpick, per the DM). The Hampshire specimen forced its way through walls: deep claw impressions in floorboards, furniture crushed inward, blood on the ceiling. Kills are **crushed, mauled, half-eaten** — not drained.

## Variants

- **Sighted** — destroyed Valerian's family home in [[Hampshire]] (estimated 3–5 ran with the feral pack).
- **Blind** — hospital dwellers: eyeless, hunting by **sound and heat**. Immune to sight-based tricks, but **back away from strong heat/light** (Valerian's Daylight). Sunlight imposes disadvantage on vampires generally; the blind ones merely "feel the heat."

## Combat Capabilities (S02, observed)

- Multiattack (bite + claw); hits observed at 7–8, 14, 17, and 20 damage (2d8/2d10-class)
- One attack carries a **Strength save rider → knocked prone** (DC unstated; Valerian failed on an 11)
- Wall-jumping mobility (leapt off a wall at Barrett)
- Silver wounds **stop their self-healing** (implying they otherwise regenerate)

## Handlers

Controlled by **Beast Masters** (light-blue magical auras; emerald-vulnerable). [[The Beast Binder]] "trains and maintains all the vampire beasts" — relationship to the Beast Master class ⚑ pending DM.

## Record
