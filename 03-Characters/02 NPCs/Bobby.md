## Profile

Really fucking old; they won't let him retire because he's too good. Bad attitude, soft center. His weapons "don't break as easy as others"; he can **enchant weapons without an Arcane Arts master**. Can **infuse weapons with silver** (anti-vampire: stops their self-healing) and **emeralds** (the only way to kill a Beastmaster). Knows the Smith-Wesson twins of old ("it's been a hot minute"). Eats his steaks well done, with ketchup. Mashed-potatoes-dipping endorsed by the table, to his face.

## The Book Commission (S02 — SECRET)

Behind a barred door, "away from prying ears": there's a **book rumored to still be in the old hospital** — magical properties, vampire history. "You'll know it when you see it… **it's got a presence**." Find it for him and the silvered gear is free. *"Just don't tell the boss."* Also wants precious jewels (break down for castle anti-vampire protection) and emeralds.
