# Ashfall Britannia — Setting Primer

> **Living document.** Original setting with no published lore — every fact here is sourced from session transcripts. Nothing is added on inference. Each entry cites its first-seen session.

## The World (as of S01)

- A **meteor struck the Earth** when the PCs were children, covering the land in **ash and a constant cloud** — "it's kind of dark all the time." *(S01)*
- After the impact, **vampires "started popping out of the woodworks."** *(S01)*
- **Humanity is on the brink of extinction**, surviving behind walls. *(S01)*
- The world is recognizably **post-modern Earth**: old-world tech exists and is salvaged (guns are common; semi-automatics exist; museums, Christmas, Assassin's Creed references in-fiction as "old world"). *(S01)*
- The calendar still tracks **Christmas** — S01 takes place 2 days before it, in winter. *(S01)*

## Society & Factions

- **The military forces** recruit volunteers to "fight the vampires and try to reclaim land so humanity can go back out into the world." *(S01)*
- **The [[Ashwardens]]** — special forces who "walk beyond the walls into places where you will not see the sun for days, where the air tastes like burnt metal." Their trial: *survive*. They retrieve supplies and old-world tech. Commanded by [[Commander Thornfall]]. *(S01)*
- Base command includes [[Commander Varan]] (deserted S01), [[Inspector Meridia Vale]], [[Lt. Hargraven|Capt./Lt. Bron Hargraven]]. *(S01)*

## The Vampires (known facts only)

- Organized into at least one **clan** with ranks — "captains" exist; [[The Beast Binder]] is one. *(S01)*
- The clan maintains **vampire beasts** ([[Feral Vampire Thrall]]s and "better" — "they don't just stop with these small ones here. Oh, we have better."). *(S01)*
- A **"Mistress of Hunger"** is invoked as the recipient of offered hearts — hierarchy/deity status unknown. *(S01)*
- Some vampires have **increased speed, climbing, and long jumps** — the reason Ashwardens carry grappling bracers. *(S01)*
- Vampire captains were believed to operate **far north**; one appearing this far south alarmed base command. *(S01)*
- Weaknesses observed: **radiant damage** is devastating (table rule: doubled); thralls' hypersensitive hearing makes **loud sound** a weapon. *(S01)*

## Geography (known)

- [[The Walled Base (Museum-Castle)]] — the party's home base. ⚑ proper name unknown. *(S01)*
- [[Ruined City District]] — the city beyond the base's main gate. ⚑ city name unknown. *(S01)*
- A north–south axis exists with scouting reports tracking vampire captains "way north." *(S01)*
