# Hampshire

> ⚑ Spelling canonized in-session after four DM fumbles (Harvershire/Hartfordshire/Hamfordshire/Parkfordshire → "Oh, Hampshire. Yeah, that's it"). First seen: [[Session 02 — Ashes of the Living]].

The little village just outside the fort ([[The Walled Base (Museum-Castle)]]) — **hit hardest in the S01 vampire attack**, and home village of [[Valerian Hellebore]] (and apparently the Smith-Wesson twins' old haunt — [[Bobby]] knows them).

## State (S02 — the morning after)

Razed and burning. Bodies either **drained bloodless** (feral thrall kills, often armed with pitchforks/kitchen knives — they fought back) or **crushed, mauled, half-eaten** (beast thrall kills). Valuables untouched — the attackers wanted people, not goods. **No children among the dead**; their tracks absent — carried out. A locket with two children's portraits recovered.

A single set of **magically-auraed footprints** (light blue — Beast Master signature; pointed shoes, slight heel, large) toured the entire village and vanished mid-step, as if its owner was picked off the ground.

## Events

- **S01:** Destroyed in the vampire assault (offscreen).
- **S02:** Party sweep — zero survivors; Valerian's parents found dead in his childhood home; Vega's pyre detail; the footprint discovery; 50 gp recovered.
