# The Grand Library of the Veiled Dawn

> First seen: [[Session 08 — Silence in the Library]]. Replaces the S07 "South Tower" heist target (DM retcon ⚑).

**"The only place left with knowledge on ancient relics and the lost history of the sun."** Massive — cathedral-high ceilings, sprawling wings, "like a dark mirror of the Library of Congress." Surprisingly *active* for a vampire-ruled world: torches on the outer columns, silhouettes past cracked windows; only broken glass and peeling murals betray its age. Scroll collections going back **10,000 years**.

## Controlled by the CULT OF THE PALE WORLD

Human devotees of the vampire overlords. Purpose: **guard forbidden knowledge, destroy anything hinting at the vampires' origins, and prevent rediscovery of how to return the sun.**

**Hierarchy** (S08 group History): priests/monks → **bishops** (2 handmaidens; the "Beast Tamer" tier ⚑) → **cardinals** (shapeshifting vampires, extra sun tolerance, most human-passable; 7 handmaidens) → **the Pope** (deals directly with the vampires; unlimited handmaidens). Area bishop: **[[Aloysius]] ("Al")**. Restricted-section keeper: **Gregory** (priest; away 2 days, destination secret ⚑). A **cardinal** haunts the region — identity unknown, tracked by "very, very gruesome religious murders" ⚑.

## Layout

- **Floor 1:** books; help desk ("second shelf to the right"); 4 spiral staircases up.
- **Floor 2:** scrolls + magic tomes (sections by age: 5,000 yr / 10,000 yr / history books / magic scrolls).
- **Floor 3:** **THE RESTRICTED SECTION** — iron gate + **electrified magic bars** (anti-pass-through ward; feedback damage); a **3×6-pin lock**; scroll archive room (monks "scroll-scrying" recent acquisitions); meeting room with **blood-spotted table, some spots fresh** ⚑; private quarters; the Watch Captain's office; a religious room (pews + small altars) onto a rear balcony; a back terrace door.
- **The hidden bookshelf entrance:** red/green/blue tomes into the correct 3 of 8 slots — wrong slot rings a chime; worn triggers on slots 1, 3, 5, 8.

## House Rules of the House

**NO MAGIC** — enforced by ear (verbal components draw an instant "*shh, no magic*"). No coughing either. Guards: ~15 cultists, door guard (Josh), roaming patrols on a 2.5-minute circuit — "not counting any vampire thralls or beast thralls, and now that I've said something, I won't tell you if there are."

Restricted-section doors **smashed open** (Vega's greataxe + Valerian's nat-20 Starry Wisp); **entire library alerted**; ~15 combatants converging. Five books taken (red/Broken Shards, "sun," "vampire," green humming, blue whispering) + the human-skin book. S09 opens here, mid-alarm.
