# Ruined City District

> ⚑ City/district name unknown — descriptive title. First seen: [[Session 01 — Graduation Day]].

The city immediately beyond the main gate of [[The Walled Base (Museum-Castle)]]. Partially ruined; civilians still live (or lived) here.

## Atmosphere (S01)

Snowfall, eerie silence "like when it's snowing and it's just quiet." Smoke curling over the district; **fog clinging unnaturally low, swirling** — used as concealment by vampires. Things move on rooftops at the edge of vision. A ruined plaza lies within sight of the gate.

## Events

- **S01:** [[Feral Vampire Thrall]] attack (two waves fought, third recalled); [[The Beast Binder]]'s first appearance at the far edge of the ruined plaza; civilian rescue and gate-lever retrieval.
