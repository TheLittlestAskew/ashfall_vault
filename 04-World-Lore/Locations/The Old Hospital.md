# The Old Hospital

> ⚑ Proper name unknown — descriptive title. First seen: [[Session 02 — Ashes of the Living]].

Two hours east of the fort. The party visited as children (pre-collapse). Two stories **plus a basement (the morgue)**. "The hospital looks like a corpse refusing burial."

## Known Features (S02)

- Chain-link perimeter fence (party cut through the wire; gates chained)
- One large blood smear up the front steps — something dragged inside
- First floor = emergency room: flickering ceiling lamps, overturned metal beds, skeletons and bodies in varying decay, dirt and leaves
- Supply room (floor 1): blankets, candles, emergency flashlights — nothing medical
- A **hole in the ER floor leading to the basement**
- Pharmacy (not yet reached) — should stock **nirnroot**
- Administration (not yet reached) — likely location of [[Bobby]]'s **book**

## Inhabitants

- **3 blind [[Beast Thrall]]s** — slain S02 (first floor)
- Heavy footsteps, bed-scraping, and *breathing in another wing* heard on arrival — **something else is still here**

## Second Floor (S03)

Nurses' dormitory: meteor-era bloodstains, cobwebs, dust, overturned gurneys, barrels. **The Beast Master's boot-prints appear here too** — he toured the hospital as well as Hampshire. Inhabited by feral + beast thralls (wall-jumpers, including blind-variant behavior unconfirmed).
